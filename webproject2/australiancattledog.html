World's longest-living dog

(not pictured)

Bluey, the Guinness World Record holder for longest-living dog, lived to be 29 years and 5 months.
He was an Australian Cattle Dog who lived on a farm in Victoria, Australia.
The average lifespan of an Australian Cattle Dog is 13-15 years.
