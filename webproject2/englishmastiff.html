World's longest dog
Zorba, the Guinness World Record holder for world's longest dog, was 8 ft and 3 inches tall.
He also was the world record holder for world's heaviest dog at 345 pounds.
He was an Old English Mastiff from London, England who lived from 1981 to 1992.
