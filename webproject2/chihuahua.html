World's smallest dog

Miracle Milly is the Guinness World Record holder for smallest dog by height, at 3.8 inches tall.
She is a Chihuahua from Dorado, Puerto Rico and weighs approximately 1 pound.
The pet cloning company Sooam has made 49 clones of her.
